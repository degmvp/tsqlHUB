Bitwise Operator
---------------------------------
~ Bitwise unary NOT
& Bitwise AND
| Bitwise OR
^ Bitwise XOR
>> Shift Right
<< Shift left
& = Bitwise AND Assignment
|=  Bitwise OR  Assignment
^=  Bitwise XOR Assignment
>>= Shift Right Assignment
<<= Shift Left  Assignment
----------------------------------
----------------------------------
The Left Shift
 byte a=8, b=24;
int c;
c=a<<2; 00001000 << 2 = 00100000=32
-----------------------------------
-----------------------------------
The Right Shift
 byte a=8, b=24;
int c ;
c=a>>2; 00001000 >> 2= 00000010=2
-----------------------------------
